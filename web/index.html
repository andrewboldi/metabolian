The signature

The grammar of metabolism

A chart is only as good as its arrows. Every relationship in Metabolian resolves to exactly one stroke from a single registry — so a glance separates catalysis from inhibition, transport from transcription. The renderer, this legend and the schema all read the same file.

Glossary

Method

Grounded, not guessed

Metabolism is too important to hallucinate. Every reaction, regulator and structure carries provenance — the database record or paper it came from — and an honest confidence level you can see and audit. Where the literature disagrees, the atlas shows the disagreement rather than smoothing it over.

Reactions are ingested from Rhea and cross-referenced through MetaNetX; every metabolite resolves to a ChEBI identifier, so a compound means the same thing on every sheet it appears on.
